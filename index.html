Anambra election APC candidate, Andy Uba, rejects outcome of poll

The candidate of the All Peogressives congress in the just concluded Anambra state Governorship Election, Senator Andy Uba, has rejected the outcome of the polls.

he described the result as declared by the independent national election commission "as a charade" which was not reflective of the wishes and aspirations of the people of the state.
