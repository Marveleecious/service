THE SCHOOL

El-shaddai Comprehensive High-Schools has been in operation for over 25 years as a school. Since its inception and till now, God has been with us all the way. For without him we can do nothing. The creation of Osun State with Osogbo as its headquarters in 1991 has continued to generate an influx of families desiring independent high quality educational facilities at foundation level, for children. Was established to facilitate excellence in elementary education in Osun State, with a curriculum developed from sound educational theories and a thorough assessment of several international educational standards including those of the International Baccalaureate Organization (IBO) Standards.
